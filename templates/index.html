Understanding Mental Health Metrics

Stress Levels

  • Normal (0.0 - 0.25): Balanced daily stress
  • Moderate (0.26 - 0.50): Manageable tension
  • High (0.51 - 0.75): Significant pressure
  • Severe (0.76 - 1.0): Critical stress level

Activity Balance

  • Optimal Study: 6-8 hours daily
  • Healthy Sleep: 7-9 hours daily
  • Social Media: 1-2 hours daily
  • Deadlines: 3-5 manageable
